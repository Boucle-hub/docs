Managing your Ruffus subscription should be straightforward. Below you will find answers to the most common billing questions. If you cannot find what you are looking for, reach out to the team directly at [billing@ruffus.app](mailto:billing@ruffus.app).

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Do I need a credit card to start the free trial?">
    No. Your 14-day free trial starts the moment you create your account — no credit card or payment information required. You only need to add a payment method if you decide to continue with a paid plan once the trial ends.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I cancel anytime?">
    Yes. Ruffus is a no-contract service, so you can cancel your subscription at any time from your dashboard under **Settings → Billing → Cancel subscription**. Your plan remains active until the end of the current billing period, and you will not be charged again after that.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Is there a long-term contract?">
    No. Ruffus operates on a month-to-month basis — there is no long-term commitment. If you choose annual billing to take advantage of 2 months free, your subscription runs for one year, but you are never locked in beyond that billing cycle.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How does annual billing work?">
    When you switch to annual billing, Ruffus gives you **2 months free** — an instant saving compared to paying monthly. The full amount is billed upfront at the start of each billing year. To switch, go to **Settings → Billing → Change billing cycle** and select **Annual**. The change takes effect at your next renewal date.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How do I upgrade or downgrade my plan?">
    Open **Settings → Billing** in your dashboard and select **Change plan**. Choose your new plan and confirm — the change takes effect immediately. When you upgrade, you gain access to new features right away. When you downgrade, your new rate applies at the start of the next billing period.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I change plans mid-month?">
    Yes. You can switch plans at any time — you are not locked in until the end of a billing cycle. If you upgrade mid-month, Ruffus prorates the charge so you only pay for the remainder of the current period at the new rate. If you downgrade, the lower rate kicks in at the start of your next billing period.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Do you offer refunds?">
    Refund requests are handled on a case-by-case basis. If you believe you are entitled to a refund, contact the billing team at [billing@ruffus.app](mailto:billing@ruffus.app) and include your account email and the reason for your request. The team will review your situation and respond as quickly as possible.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What payment methods do you accept?">
    Ruffus accepts all major credit and debit cards, including Visa, Mastercard, and American Express. Payments are processed securely. If you have specific payment requirements — such as invoicing for an annual Custom plan — contact [billing@ruffus.app](mailto:billing@ruffus.app) to discuss your options.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How do I update my billing information?">
    Go to **Settings → Billing → Payment method** in your dashboard. From there you can add a new card, update an existing one, or change the card used for future charges. Updates take effect immediately and apply to your next scheduled payment.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What happens when my trial ends?">
    At the end of your 14-day free trial, your account transitions to the paid plan you selected during sign-up. If you added a payment method, your first charge processes automatically. If you have not added a payment method, your loyalty program will be paused until you complete billing setup — your data is never deleted. You can add a payment method under **Settings → Billing** at any time to reactivate your account.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
