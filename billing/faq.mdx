Managing your Ruffus subscription should be straightforward. Below you will find answers to the most common billing questions. If you cannot find what you are looking for, reach out to the team directly at [billing@ruffus.app](mailto:billing@ruffus.app).

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Do I need a credit card to start the free trial?">
    No. Your 14-day free trial starts the moment you create your account — no credit card or payment information required. You only need to add a payment method if you decide to continue with a paid plan once the trial ends.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I cancel anytime?">
    Yes. Ruffus is a no-contract service, so you can cancel your subscription at any time from your dashboard's **Billing** page. Your plan remains active until the end of the current billing period, and you will not be charged again after that. If you change your mind before the period ends, you can reactivate from the same page.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Is there a long-term contract?">
    No. Ruffus operates on a month-to-month basis — there is no long-term commitment. If you choose annual billing to take advantage of 2 months free, your subscription runs for one year, but you are never locked in beyond that billing cycle.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How does annual billing work?">
    When you switch to annual billing, Ruffus gives you **2 months free** — an instant saving compared to paying monthly. The full amount is billed upfront at the start of each billing year. To switch, go to **Billing** in your dashboard and select the annual option for your plan. The change takes effect immediately, with the unused portion of your current period prorated on the invoice.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How do I upgrade or downgrade my plan?">
    Open **Billing** in your dashboard and select **Change plan**. Upgrades take effect immediately, with the difference prorated on your next invoice. Downgrades are scheduled for the end of your current billing period — you keep your current plan until then, and the new price applies from your next renewal. You can cancel a scheduled downgrade from **Billing** any time before it takes effect. If you downgrade below your current number of active locations, you'll choose which locations stay active; the others are deactivated at renewal (not deleted).
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I change plans mid-month?">
    Yes. You can switch plans at any time — you are not locked in until the end of a billing cycle. If you upgrade mid-month, Ruffus prorates the charge so you only pay for the remainder of the current period at the new rate. If you downgrade, you keep your current plan through the end of the period you've already paid for, and the lower rate kicks in at the start of your next billing period.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Do you offer refunds?">
    Refund requests are handled on a case-by-case basis. If you believe you are entitled to a refund, contact the billing team at [billing@ruffus.app](mailto:billing@ruffus.app) and include your account email and the reason for your request. The team will review your situation and respond as quickly as possible.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What payment methods do you accept?">
    Ruffus accepts major credit and debit cards, processed securely by Stripe. If you have specific payment requirements — such as invoicing for an annual Custom plan — contact [billing@ruffus.app](mailto:billing@ruffus.app) to discuss your options.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How do I update my billing information?">
    Go to **Billing** in your dashboard and select **Payment method**. From there you can add a new card, update an existing one, or change the card used for future charges. Updates take effect immediately and apply to your next scheduled payment.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What happens if a payment fails?">
    If a renewal charge fails — for example, an expired or declined card — your loyalty program is paused: stamps, redemptions, and campaigns are unavailable until billing is resolved. Your data and your customers' cards are never deleted. Update your card under **Billing → Payment method** in your dashboard; once the payment goes through, everything resumes automatically.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What happens when my trial ends?">
    At the end of your 14-day free trial, your account transitions to the paid plan you selected during sign-up. If you added a payment method, your first charge processes automatically. If you have not added a payment method, your account is marked as canceled about a week after the trial ends, followed by a **30-day grace period**. During the grace period you can export your member list (CSV) at any time, and you'll receive a warning email before anything is removed. After the 30 days, your loyalty data is permanently deleted. To keep your program running, subscribe from your dashboard's **Billing** page any time before then.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
