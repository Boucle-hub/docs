## Annual billing — get 2 months free

When you pay annually, Ruffus gives you **2 months free** compared to the monthly rate. Your subscription is charged as a single upfront payment at the start of each billing year. You can switch from monthly to annual billing at any time from your dashboard under **Settings → Billing**.

## Start your free trial

Every new account begins with a **14-day free trial** — no credit card required, no commitment. To get started:

1. Go to [ruffus.app/auth/signup](https://ruffus.app/auth/signup).
2. Create your account and follow the setup wizard to configure your first store.
3. Explore every feature on the plan you select — your trial includes the full feature set.
4. Before your trial ends, add a payment method to keep your loyalty program running without interruption.

## Upgrade or change your plan

You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time directly from your Ruffus dashboard:

1. Open **Settings → Billing**.
2. Select **Change plan**.
3. Choose your new plan and confirm. Changes take effect immediately.

If you are on Basic and need to manage more than one location, upgrading to Growth takes only a few clicks — your existing stamps, members, and campaign history carry over automatically.

## Need more than 3 stores?

If your business runs more than 3 locations, the Custom plan gives you flexible multi-location syncing and centralized brand management tailored to your needs. [Get in touch with the Ruffus team](https://ruffus.app/en/contact) to discuss a setup that works for you.
