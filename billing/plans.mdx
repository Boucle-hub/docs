## Annual billing

Paying annually gets you **2 months free** compared to paying monthly:

- Basic: $499.90 CAD/yr (equivalent to $41.65/mo)
- Growth: $999.90 CAD/yr (equivalent to $83.32/mo)

Annual plans are billed upfront for the full year. You can switch between monthly and annual billing anytime — the change takes effect immediately, with prorated billing for the switch.

## Free trial

Every new account gets a **14-day free trial** with full feature access — no credit card required to start. Use the trial to run Ruffus with real customers before picking a plan.

<Warning>
  If your trial ends without a payment method on file, your account is marked canceled about a week later, then enters a 30-day grace period. You can export your member list as a CSV during the grace period, and you'll get a warning email about a week before anything is removed. After the full 30 days, your loyalty data is permanently deleted. Add a payment method before the trial ends to avoid all of this.
</Warning>

## Changing plans

**Upgrades** — moving to a higher plan, or switching monthly → annual — take effect **immediately**: you get the new features right away, and the difference is prorated so you only pay for the remainder of the current period at the new rate.

**Downgrades** — moving to a lower plan, or switching annual → monthly — are **scheduled for the end of your current billing period**. You keep everything you've already paid for until then, and the new plan and price apply from your next renewal. Changed your mind? You can cancel a scheduled downgrade from **Billing** any time before it takes effect.

If you downgrade below your current location count (for example, Growth's 3 locations down to Basic's 1), you'll choose which locations stay active when you schedule the downgrade. The others are deactivated at renewal — not deleted — so their data is preserved if you add locations back later. See [Multi-Location](/dashboard/multi-location) for details.

## Cancelling

You can cancel anytime from **Dashboard → Billing**. Your program stays active through the end of your current paid period — cancelling doesn't cut you off immediately. If you're on an annual plan, cancelling stops the renewal; there's no partial refund for the unused months of the year. A pause option is also available if you want a break without fully cancelling. You can reactivate anytime before your period ends; after a full cancellation, you'd resubscribe through checkout.

Refunds outside the normal cancellation flow are handled case by case — reach out to [billing@ruffus.app](mailto:billing@ruffus.app).

## Payment methods

Ruffus accepts major credit and debit cards, processed securely through Stripe. Ruffus never stores your card number.
