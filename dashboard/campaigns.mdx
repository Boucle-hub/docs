Ruffus keeps customers engaged in two ways: promos you run yourself from **Dashboard → Campaigns**, and a set of lifecycle messages Ruffus sends automatically, with no setup required. This page covers both.

## Running your own campaigns

From **Dashboard → Campaigns**, you can launch:

- **Bonus stamps** — customers earn extra stamps (up to 20) on top of the normal one per approved stamp request, for a limited time.
- **New-member bonus** — customers who join during the promo window get a stamp-count head start.

Both can be scoped to a specific [location](/dashboard/multi-location) and a specific [card](/loyalty-card/multiple-cards), with a start and end date — handy for a seasonal push without touching your permanent program rules.

<Tip>
  For an always-on reward instead of a limited-time promo, see [VIP Tiers](/loyalty-card/tiers) — tiers grant a standing bonus-stamp perk to loyal customers rather than everyone, for a limited window.
</Tip>

## Automatic lifecycle messages

Ruffus also sends a set of automatic emails and push notifications on your behalf — nothing to build, nothing to send. Most aren't configurable; two are.

| Message | Sent when | Frequency | Configurable? |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Welcome | A customer joins your card | Once, on joining | No |
| First stamp | A customer earns their very first stamp | Once | No |
| Milestone | A customer crosses a lifetime-stamp milestone (10, 25, 50, 100) | Once per milestone | No |
| [Reward ready](/loyalty-card/wallet) | A customer completes their card | Once per completed card | No |
| Reward reminder | A completed reward sits unclaimed for 14\+ days | Once per reward | No |
| Redemption follow-up | Right after a customer redeems a reward | Once per redemption | No |
| Expiration reminder | A customer's earned reward is approaching its expiry (see below) | Once per reward | Indirectly — via your reward-expiry setting |
| Slow-day nudge | A day of the week you've marked as historically quiet | Weekly, on the day(s) you pick | **Yes** — off by default |
| Anniversary | The anniversary of a customer's join date | Once a year | No |
| Birthday | A customer's self-reported birthday (set from their own account) | Once a year | No |
| Win-back | A customer hasn't visited in 30\+ days | At most once every 60 days | **Yes** — personal note |

All of these send by email and push notification together, same as the rest of Ruffus's customer messaging.

### Win-back's personal note

The one thing you can customize on the win-back message is a short personal note, up to 280 characters, appended to the email. Find it at **Dashboard → Campaigns → Win-back**, alongside an audience preview and send history. See [Members](/dashboard/customers#win-back-emails-automatic) for the full win-back picture.

### Slow-day nudges

Unlike everything else in this table, the slow-day nudge is entirely opt-in: pick which day (or days) of the week tend to be quiet for your business, and Ruffus sends a nudge to recently-active-but-not-regular customers on those days. It's off until you turn it on — there's no default day.

### Reward expiry and expiration reminders

By default, an earned reward never expires. From **Settings → Program rules**, you can optionally set an expiry window (7, 14, 30, 60, 90, 180, or 365 days) after which an unredeemed reward becomes void. If you turn this on, Ruffus automatically sends the expiration-reminder message above while there's still time to redeem.

<Note>
  Send history for anniversary, expiration, slow-day, and both reward messages is visible from **Dashboard → Campaigns → Win-back**'s history panel, alongside win-back sends — it's the one place to see recent automated sends across message types.
</Note>
