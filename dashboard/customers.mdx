Your customers list is where the loyalty data gets personal. Rather than seeing aggregate numbers alone, you can drill down into individual customer behaviour — who visits most often, when they were last in, and how many stamps they have accumulated. This level of detail helps you recognise your regulars by name and make informed decisions about how to reward and re-engage them.

## The Customers List

Navigate to **Customers** in the left sidebar to open the full member list. Each row in the table represents one loyalty member and displays:

| Column | What it shows |
|---|---|
| **Customer** | Display name or anonymised identifier |
| **Total Stamps** | Cumulative stamps earned across all visits |
| **Visits** | Number of times the customer has scanned their card |
| **Last Seen** | Date of their most recent visit |
| **Rewards Redeemed** | Total rewards claimed to date |

Click any customer row to open their individual profile, which shows a full timeline of stamp events and redemptions.

## Identifying Your Best Customers

Use the **Sort** control at the top of the customers list to reorder by visits or by total stamps earned. Your most engaged members rise to the top, giving you a clear view of who your loyalty programme is working hardest for. Knowing your best customers by name — and understanding how often and when they visit — lets you personalise your service and make those customers feel genuinely valued.

<Tip>
  Sort by **Last Seen** (oldest first) to quickly identify members who are overdue a visit. This is a good starting point before launching a win-back campaign.
</Tip>

## Email Campaigns

Ruffus lets you send email messages directly to your loyalty members from the dashboard — no third-party email tool required.

To send a campaign:

1. Go to **Customers → Campaigns** in the sidebar.
2. Select **New Campaign**.
3. Choose your audience: all members, members who haven't visited recently, or members who have redeemed at least once.
4. Write your subject line and message body.
5. Preview the email, then select **Send Campaign**.

Campaigns are a practical way to announce a new reward, share a seasonal promotion, or simply remind members that you appreciate their support.

## Automated Win-Back Emails

A win-back email is sent automatically to any member who hasn't visited your store within a period you define. It nudges lapsed customers back through the door without requiring any manual effort on your part.

To enable win-back emails:

1. Go to **Customers → Automations**.
2. Toggle **Win-Back Email** to on.
3. Set the inactivity threshold — for example, 30, 60, or 90 days since the customer's last visit.
4. Customise the email subject and body, or use the default template Ruffus provides.
5. Select **Save**.

Once enabled, Ruffus evaluates member activity daily and dispatches the win-back email to any customer who crosses the inactivity threshold.

<Note>
  Win-back emails include an unsubscribe link by default, in line with email marketing regulations. Members who unsubscribe will not receive future campaign or automated emails.
</Note>

## Staff Access

Ruffus distinguishes between two levels of access for your team:

**Terminal access** — gives a staff member the ability to scan stamps and process reward redemptions at the point of sale. This is the appropriate level for floor staff and baristas. They do not see any customer data or analytics.

**Dashboard access** — gives a staff member full visibility of the analytics dashboard, customer list, and campaign tools. Grant this level only to managers or owners who need to analyse performance or send communications.

To add a staff member:

1. Go to **Settings → Team**.
2. Select **Invite Staff Member**.
3. Enter their email address and choose their access level: **Terminal** or **Dashboard**.
4. Select **Send Invite**.

The invited staff member receives an email prompting them to set up their credentials. You can revoke access at any time from the same **Team** settings page.

## Privacy

Your dashboard data is private and visible only to you and any team members you explicitly invite. Customer data — including visit history and contact details — is processed in accordance with [Ruffus's privacy policy](https://ruffus.app/privacy). Ruffus does not share individual customer data with third parties, and customers can request deletion of their data at any time through the Ruffus app.
