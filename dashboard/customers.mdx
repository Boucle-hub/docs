Every person who joins your loyalty card shows up in **Members** — searchable, sortable, and exportable, with a full history for each individual customer.

## The Members list

Each row shows:

- **Email** (masked for privacy — you'll see something like `j***@gmail.com`, not the full address)
- **Lifetime stamps** and **current stamps** (their progress toward the next reward)
- **Last seen** and **joined** dates
- **Rewards redeemed**

You can search the list and sort by lifetime stamps, last seen, or joined date — handy for finding your most loyal customers or anyone who's gone quiet.

## Member detail timeline

Click into any member to see their full activity timeline: every stamp they've earned and every reward they've redeemed, in order. It's the fastest way to answer "has this customer actually been coming in?" during a support conversation.

## Exporting your member list

You can export your Members list as a CSV, up to 5,000 rows at a time. If you need activity over a specific date range instead — stamps, approvals, redemptions — the **Activity** page has its own CSV export with date-range filtering.

<Tip>
  Keep an export handy. If your subscription ever lapses, your CSV export from Members is also how you'd pull your customer list during the grace period — see [Plans & Billing](/billing/plans).
</Tip>

## Win-back emails (automatic)

Ruffus automatically brings inactive customers back for you — there's nothing to configure to turn this on:

- If a customer hasn't visited in **30+ days**, they get an automatic win-back email and push notification.
- A customer won't be re-nudged more than once every 60 days, so nobody gets spammed.
- All marketing messages are consent-gated with an unsubscribe link, in line with Québec's CASL rules.

The one thing you _can_ customize is a short personal note — up to 280 characters — that gets appended to the win-back email. You'll find it under **Dashboard → Campaigns → Win-back**, alongside an audience preview and send history.

<Note>
  There's no threshold selector (you can't change the 30-day window) and no template editor. The win-back email is designed and sent automatically — your customization is limited to that personal note.
</Note>

Ruffus also sends two other automatic emails, unrelated to inactivity: a "your reward is ready" email the moment a card is completed, and a reminder if an earned reward sits unclaimed for 14+ days.

## Campaigns

For promos beyond win-back, head to **Dashboard → Campaigns**. You can run bonus-stamp or new-member-bonus promotions, scoped to a specific location and a start/end date. See [Multi-Location](/dashboard/multi-location) if you're running campaigns across more than one location.

## Staff access to members

Only **owners** see the Members list and member details — managers are limited to the counter tools, and staff working the counter don't have dashboard logins at all. Staff use 4-digit PINs created at **Dashboard → Staff**, which only gate stamp approvals and redemptions, not access to your customer data. There's no email-invite flow for staff; PINs are the whole model.

## Privacy

Member data is private to your account. Ruffus operates under Québec's Law 25, and customers can request erasure of their own record at any time — it's self-serve from their account, no need to email you.
