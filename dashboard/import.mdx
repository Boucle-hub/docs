If you already keep a customer list — from a spreadsheet, an old POS export, or another loyalty tool — you can bring it into Ruffus instead of starting from an empty Members list.

<Note>
  Import is owner-only, found at **Dashboard → Import**.
</Note>

## How it works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Choose the destination">
    Pick which **location** and which **card** the imported customers should be enrolled in.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Upload your CSV">
    Upload your customer list. Each row becomes a customer record and a membership on the card you selected.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review and confirm">
    Ruffus creates the customers and their starting memberships in bulk.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## After importing

Imported customers show up in [Members](/dashboard/customers) like any other customer — searchable, exportable, and eligible for win-back and other automatic messages once they meet the same criteria as an organically-joined customer.

<Tip>
  Import is a one-time head start, not an ongoing sync — there's no live connection back to your original spreadsheet or POS. If you need to bring in more customers later, check with [hello@ruffus.app](mailto:hello@ruffus.app) before re-running a file that overlaps your existing Members list.
</Tip>
