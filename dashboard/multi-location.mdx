Ruffus supports running one loyalty program across multiple physical locations, with per-location and consolidated views. How many locations you can add depends on your plan.

## Location limits by plan

| Plan | Locations | Terminals per location |
|---|---|---|
| Basic — $49.99 CAD/mo | 1 | 2 |
| Growth — $99.99 CAD/mo | Up to 3 | 3 |
| Custom | 4+ (unlimited) | Custom |

Custom-plan pricing is set per contract — reach out to [hello@ruffus.app](mailto:hello@ruffus.app) to talk through your needs.

## Adding a location

<Steps>
  <Step title="Upgrade if needed">
    If you're on Basic, upgrade to Growth (or Custom) first, from **Dashboard → Billing**. Plan changes take effect immediately, with prorated billing.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add the location">
    Go to **Settings → Locations → Add**, and fill in the new location's details.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set up its staff portal">
    Each location gets its own staff portal QR code from the welcome package, so stamp requests route to the right counter.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Location filter on your overview

Once you have two or more locations, a location filter appears on the **Dashboard** overview screen, letting you switch between a **consolidated** view (all locations combined) and a **per-location** view. Growth and Custom plans also get per-location analytics breakdowns in addition to the consolidated numbers.

## Staff portals and QR variants

Every location has its own staff portal and staff QR code — staff at one location approve stamp requests for that location, not others. If a location has multiple staff devices working the counter at once, each device gets its own QR variant so stamp-request push notifications route to the right device instead of all of them at once.

There's no email-invite flow here either — staff access is entirely PIN-based, set up per staff member at **Dashboard → Staff**.

## Unified vs. granular mode

Under **Settings**, you can choose how tightly your locations are managed:

- **Unified mode** — program rules, card design, and settings apply the same way across every location.
- **Granular mode** — individual locations can diverge where it makes sense (for example, different program-rule tweaks per site).

## Downgrading below your location count

If you move from Growth to Basic (or reduce your location count on a Custom plan), you'll be asked to choose which location stays active. The others are deactivated, not deleted — their data is preserved if you add locations back later.

## What Custom adds

Beyond unlimited locations, the Custom plan includes enterprise features like custom wallet certificates, plus contact-based pricing built around how you operate. Get in touch at [hello@ruffus.app](mailto:hello@ruffus.app) to scope it out.
