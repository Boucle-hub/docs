If your business spans more than one address, Ruffus grows with you. Whether you're opening a second location or managing a large group of stores, the platform gives you a consolidated view of loyalty activity across every site while still letting you zoom in on how each individual location is performing. The level of multi-location functionality available to you depends on your plan.

## Basic Plan: Single Location

The Basic plan gives one store access to the full Ruffus analytics suite — member counts, stamp activity, visit trends, redemption rates, and customer profiles. Everything you need to run a loyalty programme for a single site is included at no extra charge.

## Growth Plan: Up to Three Locations

Upgrade to the Growth plan at **$79.99 per month** to connect up to three store locations under one account. Once multiple locations are active, your dashboard gains two new views:

- **Consolidated overview** — total members, stamps, rewards, and visit trends aggregated across all your locations in one single summary.
- **Per-location breakdown** — the same metrics sliced individually for each store, so you can compare performance and identify which locations need attention.

This makes it straightforward to answer questions like "which of my three cafés has the highest redemption rate?" or "is my new location attracting as many loyalty members as my original site?"

## Custom Plan: Four or More Locations

For groups operating four or more stores, the Custom plan provides everything in Growth plus centralised brand management and custom multi-location syncing. You can enforce a consistent loyalty card design and reward structure across all locations from a single control panel, while still drilling down into granular per-site data. Contact the Ruffus team at [ruffus.app/contact](https://ruffus.app/contact) to discuss pricing and configuration for your group.

## Adding a Second Location

To add a new store location to your account, you first need to be on the Growth plan (or Custom). Follow these steps:

1. Log in at [ruffus.app](https://ruffus.app) and go to **Settings → Subscription**.
2. Select **Upgrade to Growth** and complete the billing steps.
3. Once your plan is active, navigate to **Settings → Locations**.
4. Select **Add Location**.
5. Enter your second store's name, address, and any location-specific details.
6. Select **Save Location**.

Your new location appears immediately in the sidebar. Assign staff members to it via **Settings → Team**, and the location's terminal is ready to start collecting stamps.

<Note>
  Each location has its own terminal access credentials. Staff at Location A cannot process redemptions on behalf of Location B unless they are explicitly granted access to both.
</Note>

## Using the Consolidated Dashboard

Once you have two or more active locations, the dashboard home displays consolidated totals by default. A **Location** filter at the top of the page lets you switch between:

- **All Locations** — aggregated figures for your entire operation.
- **[Location Name]** — isolated figures for a single store.

All charts, customer lists, and campaign tools respond to the active location filter. When **All Locations** is selected, the customers list merges members across sites and indicates which location each member was first seen at.

<Tip>
  Use the per-location visit trends chart to identify if one store has a noticeably different peak time from your others. Staggering staff schedules or running location-specific promotions can help balance demand across your sites.
</Tip>
