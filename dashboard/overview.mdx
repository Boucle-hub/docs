The Ruffus merchant dashboard is your command centre for understanding how your loyalty programme is performing. From the moment a customer collects their first stamp to the day they redeem a reward, every interaction is captured and surfaced here — so you always know what's working, who's coming back, and where there's room to grow.

## Accessing the Dashboard

Log in at [ruffus.app](https://ruffus.app) using your merchant credentials. Once authenticated, you land directly on your dashboard home, which displays a live snapshot of your programme's performance. Bookmark the URL so your team can reach it quickly.

## At a Glance: Your Key Metrics

At the top of the dashboard, three summary cards give you an immediate read on the health of your loyalty programme.

<CardGroup cols={3}>
  <Card title="Members" icon="users">
    The total number of customers who have joined your loyalty programme. Watch this number grow as more people scan your card for the first time.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Stamps" icon="stamp">
    The cumulative number of stamps issued across all visits. This reflects how actively your customers are engaging with your programme.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Rewards" icon="gift">
    The total rewards redeemed by your customers. A high redemption count means customers are completing stamp cycles and coming back to claim their benefit.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Visit Trends Chart

The visit trends chart plots how often customers visit your store over a selected time window. Use the date range selector to zoom into a specific period — for example, to measure the impact of a promotion or a seasonal shift in traffic. Peaks in the chart align with high-stamp days, so you can correlate busy periods with real-world events like a local market or a sale.

## Redemption Rate

Your redemption rate tells you what percentage of completed stamp cycles actually result in a reward being claimed. A high redemption rate signals that customers value your reward and are motivated to return for it. A low rate may indicate that customers are dropping off before completing their card — a useful prompt to reconsider your stamp target or reward offering.

<Note>
  Redemption rate is calculated as: **(total rewards redeemed ÷ total stamp cycles completed) × 100**. A cycle is completed when a customer reaches the stamp threshold you set for your card.
</Note>

## Recent Activity Feed

Below the summary cards, the recent activity feed shows a live log of stamp collections and reward redemptions as they happen. Each entry displays the time of the event, the action taken (stamp or redemption), and an anonymised customer reference. Use this feed to verify that your terminal is scanning correctly during busy service periods.

## Real-Time Data

Every scan and redemption updates your dashboard instantly. There is no need to refresh the page manually — figures adjust as your staff use the Ruffus terminal throughout the day. This means you can monitor a lunchtime rush in real time or confirm that a reward was successfully redeemed moments after it happened.

<Tip>
  Keep the dashboard open on a tablet or back-office screen during trading hours. Real-time updates make it easy to spot unusual activity — such as a spike in redemptions — as it unfolds.
</Tip>
