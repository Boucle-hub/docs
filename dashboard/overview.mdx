Everything you do to run your loyalty program happens at [app.ruffus.app](https://app.ruffus.app) — your Ruffus dashboard. This page walks through the navigation and what you'll find on the overview screen itself.

<Note>
  Dashboard numbers refresh about once a minute, not instantly. If a stamp was just approved at the counter, give it a moment to show up in your charts.
</Note>

## The navigation

## What's on the overview page

The **Dashboard** screen is your home base. At a glance it shows:

- **Stamps today** — how many stamps were approved so far today.
- **Member stats** — your total member count and recent growth.
- **Activity feed** — a running stream of recent stamp requests and redemptions.
- **Close to reward** — customers who are near completing their card, so you know who's about to come back for a redemption.

Scroll further and the detailed view adds:

- A **7-day chart** of stamp activity.
- A **period chart** you can switch between 7, 30, and 90-day windows.
- **Program health** — a summary of how your loyalty program is trending.
- **At-risk members** — customers whose visit pattern suggests they're drifting away, which is also what feeds the automatic win-back emails (see [Members](/dashboard/customers)).

## Owner vs. manager access

Ruffus has two dashboard roles:

- **Owner** — full access to every section listed above.
- **Manager** — limited to the counter tools: the Redeem terminal and the QR Poster page. Managers run the floor; they don't see billing, members, staff PIN management, or program-wide settings.

Staff working the counter itself don't get dashboard accounts at all — they use a 4-digit PIN, set up by the owner from **Dashboard → Staff**. See [Members](/dashboard/customers) for more on staff access.
