**Settings** is where the rest of your program's configuration lives — anything not covered by [Multi-Location](/dashboard/multi-location), [Points](/loyalty-card/points), [VIP Tiers](/loyalty-card/tiers), [Nearby Reminders](/loyalty-card/nearby-reminders), or [Campaigns](/dashboard/campaigns). All of it is owner-only.

## White-label domain

Pick a subdomain for your dashboard — `yourshop.ruffus.app` instead of the default — from **Settings**. This is cosmetic: it changes the URL your staff and you use to reach the dashboard, not where customer-facing card links live.

## Staff approval and Fast Mode

By default, every stamp requires a staff member to approve it in the staff portal — the fraud-prevention model described in [How It Works](/how-it-works). **Fast Mode**, off by default, lets a merchant skip that per-stamp approval step for faster counter service.

<Warning>
  Turning on Fast Mode removes the staff-approval check that keeps stamps tied to real, staff-confirmed visits. Only turn it on if you understand and accept that trade-off — the cooldown window still applies, but the approval gate itself is gone.
</Warning>

## Fraud monitoring

Ruffus watches for unusual patterns — things like implausible location jumps between scans or unusually fast repeat activity — and surfaces a flag on your dashboard when something looks off, so you can take a closer look. This runs automatically in the background; there's nothing to configure.

## Referrals

Turn on **customer referrals** to reward customers who bring in a friend — both the referrer and the new member get a bonus when the friend joins, at an amount you set.

<Note>
  This is separate from **Refer a Business** (also under Settings), which is how you refer other merchants to Ruffus itself — not part of your customer-facing loyalty program. See your dashboard for current referral terms.
</Note>

## Language

Set your own dashboard language independently of the trilingual FR/EN/ES program your customers see — the two aren't linked.
