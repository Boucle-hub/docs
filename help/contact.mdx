The Ruffus team is a small, remote-first group based in Québec, Canada. Whether you have a quick setup question, a billing concern, or a partnership idea, there's a direct line for each — no ticket queues or bot-only support. Replies typically land within a few hours on weekdays, and the team is happy to help in **English**, **French**, or **Spanish**.

## Response times

Support emails sent to [hello@ruffus.app](mailto:hello@ruffus.app) are answered within a few hours on weekdays (Monday–Friday, Eastern Time). Messages sent over the weekend or on public holidays are answered on the next business day. Billing and press enquiries follow the same schedule.

## Prefer a contact form?

If you'd rather not open your email client, you can also reach us through the web form at [ruffus.app/en/contact](https://ruffus.app/en/contact). Just fill in your name, email, and message — the form routes your enquiry to the right inbox automatically.
