## Setting up your card

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Where do I design my loyalty card?">
    In **Card Studio**, found at **Dashboard → QR Poster**. You can set your logo, brand color, card image, stamp shape (circle, square, triangle, diamond, star, hexagon, or heart), stamp fill style, stamp count, and reward label in French, English, and Spanish.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="If I change my stamp count, do customers lose progress?">
    No. Changing the stamp count never resets anyone's progress. If you lower the count, customers who now qualify are retroactively granted their reward.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How do I get my QR poster?">
    From **Dashboard → QR Poster**, download it as a PNG or print it directly from your browser's print dialog. The QR code encodes a permanent join link that never changes, so you only need to print it once.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Collecting stamps

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="How does a customer earn a stamp?">
    The customer scans your QR poster, which opens the join page on their phone. They sign in (phone number by SMS code, email, or Google/Apple sign-in — no app download required), which creates a stamp request with a roughly 30-second window. Your staff device gets an instant push notification, and a staff member taps **Approve** in the staff portal. Only then is the stamp added — this one-tap approval is what keeps stamps tied to real visits instead of screenshots or shared codes.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What happens if staff don't respond in time?">
    If nobody approves within about 30 seconds, the request expires and the customer can simply try again.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Why can't a customer stamp twice in a row?">
    A cooldown applies after each stamp — 60 minutes by default. It's configurable (and can be disabled entirely) in **Settings → Program rules**. If a customer scans too soon, they see a friendly cooldown screen instead of an error.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="A customer says they lost their progress — what happened?">
    Stamp progress is tied to their account, not their device. If they're not seeing their card, they most likely need to sign back in with the same phone number, email, or Google/Apple account they used originally. Progress itself isn't lost. If they're still stuck, have them contact [hello@ruffus.app](mailto:hello@ruffus.app).
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can a customer have more than one active card with us?">
    Each merchant runs one active loyalty program at a time, so a customer has one active card per business. If you want to run a limited-time promotion on top of that, use bonus-stamp or new-member-bonus promos in **Dashboard → Campaigns** rather than a separate card.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Redeeming rewards

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="How does a customer redeem their reward?">
    When their card is full, a **Redeem** button appears and generates a 6-digit code (plus a QR) they show at the counter. Staff enter or scan that code in the **Redeem terminal** — if you've enabled redemption PINs, staff also enter their 6-digit PIN. The reward is marked redeemed, the stamp count resets, and a new cycle starts. Double-redeeming the same code isn't possible.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="We redeemed a reward by mistake — can we undo it?">
    There's no self-serve undo. Contact [hello@ruffus.app](mailto:hello@ruffus.app) with the customer's name or email and the date of the redemption — if it was a genuine mistake, our team can look up the transaction and restore their stamps.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Where can I see our redemption history?">
    **Dashboard → Rewards** shows redemption KPIs, your full redemption history, and your top redeemers. For a date-range export, use the CSV export on the **Activity** page.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Staff and multi-location

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="How do I add staff?">
    There's no email-invite system. Instead, create a 6-digit PIN for each staff member at **Dashboard → Staff**. Staff use their PIN at the counter — they don't need a dashboard login at all. Dashboard access itself is limited to two roles: **owner** (full access) and **manager** (limited to the counter tools — Redeem terminal and QR Poster).
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can we run Ruffus across more than one location?">
    Yes. Basic supports 1 location, Growth supports up to 3, and Custom supports 4 or more. Each location gets its own staff portal QR code, and locations with multiple staff devices get per-device QR variants so push notifications reach the right device. See [Multi-Location](/dashboard/multi-location) for the full picture.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Analytics and devices

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Is the dashboard real-time?">
    Close to it, but not instant — dashboard numbers refresh about once a minute.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Do customers need to install an app?">
    No. Everything runs in the browser — no app download for customers or staff. Customers can optionally add their card to Apple Wallet, Google Wallet, or their phone's home screen for quicker access, and it stays automatically up to date.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Does Ruffus work offline?">
    No — every stamp is verified server-side as part of the approval flow, so an internet connection is required to request and approve stamps.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Billing

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan?">
    Yes, anytime, from **Dashboard → Billing**. Upgrades take effect immediately with prorated billing. Downgrades take effect at your next renewal — you keep your current plan until the end of the period you've paid for, and you can cancel a scheduled downgrade before then. Downgrading below your current location count will ask you to choose which locations stay active.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How does cancellation work?">
    Cancel anytime from **Dashboard → Billing**. Your program stays active through the end of your current paid period. If you're on an annual plan, cancelling stops the renewal rather than refunding the unused months.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I get a refund?">
    Refunds are handled case by case — reach out to [billing@ruffus.app](mailto:billing@ruffus.app) and we'll take a look.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
