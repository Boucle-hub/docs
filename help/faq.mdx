Whether you're just getting started or fine-tuning a loyalty programme that's already live, the answers below cover the questions merchants ask most often. If you don't find what you're looking for, head to the [contact page](/help/contact) and the team will get back to you within a few hours on weekdays.

## Getting Started

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="How do I create my first loyalty card?">
    Sign in to your Ruffus dashboard and click **Create card**. From there, give your card a name, upload your logo, choose your brand colours, set the number of stamps required, and write your reward. The whole process takes under five minutes, and no design skills are needed. When you're happy with the preview, click **Publish** — your card is live instantly and ready to share with customers.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How do customers collect stamps?">
    Customers collect stamps by scanning the QR code you display at your counter or checkout. The code opens a lightweight page in their browser — no app download required. Each scan adds one stamp to their card. If they've already added the card to Apple Wallet or Google Wallet, the stamp count updates there automatically too.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How do I display my QR code?">
    Go to **Dashboard → Your card → Share**, then click **Download QR code**. You'll get a high-resolution PNG you can print and display anywhere — a countertop stand, the back of a receipt, a window cling, or a menu. You can also share the card link digitally via social media or a messaging app. The QR code never changes, so you only need to print it once.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Stamps & Rewards

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Can I change my reward after I launch?">
    Yes — you can edit your reward description, stamp count, or any other card detail at any time from the **Card settings** page. Changes take effect immediately for new cards started after the update. Customers who already have stamps in progress will complete their current card under the original reward, so no one loses out on a promise you already made.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What if a customer loses their progress?">
    If a customer loses access to their card link (for example, they cleared their browser or got a new phone), contact Ruffus support at [hello@ruffus.app](mailto:hello@ruffus.app) with the customer's name and, if possible, their email address or phone number. The team can look up their record and restore their stamp count manually.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I run more than one card at once?">
    Yes — all Ruffus plans include unlimited active loyalty cards, so you can run separate programmes for different product lines, locations, or seasonal promotions simultaneously. Each card has its own QR code and its own stamp-and-reward configuration. You manage all of them from a single dashboard.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Billing

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="How do I upgrade or downgrade my plan?">
    Go to **Dashboard → Settings → Billing** and choose the plan that fits your needs. Upgrades take effect immediately, and you'll only be charged the prorated difference for the remainder of your billing cycle. Downgrades take effect at the start of your next billing period. For a full breakdown of what each plan includes, see the [Billing FAQ](/billing/faq).
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I cancel anytime?">
    Yes. Ruffus is month-to-month with no long-term contract. You can cancel from **Dashboard → Settings → Billing** at any time. Your account stays active until the end of the period you've already paid for. If you're on an annual plan, cancelling stops the next renewal but does not trigger a mid-year refund — see the [Billing FAQ](/billing/faq) for details.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Do you offer refunds?">
    All plans come with a 14-day free trial (no credit card required), so you can try Ruffus before committing. After that, charges are generally non-refundable, but if you believe you were billed in error, reach out to [billing@ruffus.app](mailto:billing@ruffus.app) and the team will review your case. Full details are in the [Billing FAQ](/billing/faq).
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Redemption

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="How does reward redemption work?">
    When a customer's stamp card is full, a **Redeem** button appears on their card. They show you the screen, and you tap **Confirm redemption** in the Ruffus terminal on your device. The card is then reset to zero stamps so they can start earning again right away. Every redemption is logged in your dashboard under **Redemption history**.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What if a customer redeems by accident?">
    If a redemption was confirmed by mistake, go to **Dashboard → Redemption history**, find the transaction, and tap **Reverse redemption**. Reversals are available within 24 hours of the original redemption. After that window, contact [hello@ruffus.app](mailto:hello@ruffus.app) and the team can assist with a manual correction.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I see a record of all redemptions?">
    Yes. Every redemption is logged in real time under **Dashboard → Redemption history**. You can filter by date range, location, or card, and export the data as a CSV for your own records. The Growth plan adds per-location breakdowns so you can see which outlet is driving the most reward activity.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Staff & Account

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Can I give my staff access to the terminal?">
    Yes. Go to **Dashboard → Settings → Staff** and invite team members by email. Staff accounts have terminal-only access by default — they can stamp and redeem but cannot change card settings or view billing information. You can revoke access at any time from the same page.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I run Ruffus across multiple locations?">
    Yes. The **Growth plan** supports up to three locations, each with its own terminal, QR code, and analytics. You manage everything from one account, and the dashboard shows you both a consolidated view and per-location breakdowns so you can compare performance across sites. Need more than three locations? Contact the team at [hello@ruffus.app](mailto:hello@ruffus.app) to discuss options.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What analytics does Ruffus give me?">
    Your dashboard shows new cards issued, stamps collected per day, total redemptions, and your most active time periods. The Growth plan adds per-location data and a customer return-rate metric. All analytics are updated in real time and can be exported as a CSV. No third-party analytics tools are required — everything is built in.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Technical

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Does Ruffus work on all devices?">
    Ruffus is fully browser-based, so it works on any smartphone, tablet, or desktop with a modern browser — iOS Safari, Android Chrome, Firefox, and Edge are all supported. There's no app to install for you or your customers. The merchant terminal is also browser-based, so you can run it on any device you already have at the counter.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Do customers need to create an account?">
    No. Customers do not need to sign up or create an account to collect stamps. Their card is tied to a unique link that opens directly in their browser. They can optionally add the card to Apple Wallet or Google Wallet after their first stamp for easy access — but that's entirely up to them.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Is there an offline mode?">
    At this time, Ruffus requires an internet connection to issue and record stamps, because each stamp is verified and logged on the server in real time. If your connection drops briefly, simply wait for it to restore before scanning — the process takes only a second or two once you're back online. The team is exploring offline queuing for a future update.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
