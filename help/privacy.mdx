## What data Ruffus collects

Ruffus collects only the information needed to run the service:

- **Account information** — your name, email address, and business details when you sign up as a merchant.
- **Loyalty card activity** — stamps issued, rewards redeemed, and card interactions, tied to a customer's unique card link.
- **Payment information** — billing is handled through a third-party payment provider. Ruffus never stores your full card number or sensitive payment credentials directly.
- **Device and usage data** — browser type, device type, IP address, and how you interact with the dashboard or loyalty card pages. This helps the team diagnose bugs and improve the product.

## How your data is used

Ruffus uses the information it collects to:

- Run and maintain the loyalty platform for merchants and their customers.
- Send transactional notifications (for example, a stamp confirmation or a reward alert).
- Detect and prevent fraud or abuse.
- Comply with legal obligations, including tax and financial record-keeping.

**Ruffus does not sell your personal data to advertisers or third parties — ever.**

## Your privacy rights

Depending on where you live, you have the right to:

- **Access** the personal data Ruffus holds about you.
- **Correct** any information that is inaccurate or out of date.
- **Delete** your data (subject to legal retention requirements — see below).
- **Export** your data in a portable format.
- **Withdraw consent** for any processing based on consent, at any time.
- **Lodge a complaint** with a supervisory authority, such as the Commission d'accès à l'information (CAI) in Québec or the Office of the Privacy Commissioner of Canada (OPC).

To exercise any of these rights, email [privacy@ruffus.app](mailto:privacy@ruffus.app) with your request. The Privacy Officer will respond promptly.

## How long data is kept

Ruffus retains your data for as long as your account is active, plus **90 days after account closure** — giving you time to request an export before anything is removed. Transaction and financial records are kept for up to **7 years** to meet accounting and tax compliance requirements under applicable law.

## Compliance

Ruffus is designed to comply with:

- **Québec Law 25** (Act to modernize legislative provisions as regards the protection of personal information)
- **PIPEDA** (Personal Information Protection and Electronic Documents Act, Canada)
- **CASL** (Canada's Anti-Spam Legislation)
- **GDPR** (General Data Protection Regulation, European Union)

All data is protected with industry-standard encryption in transit and at rest. In the event of a data breach, Ruffus will notify the CAI and all affected individuals as required by Law 25.

## Children

Ruffus is not directed at children under the age of 14. If you are under 14, parental or guardian consent is required before using the service.

## Contact the Privacy Officer

For any privacy-related questions or to submit a data rights request, contact the Ruffus Privacy Officer directly at [privacy@ruffus.app](mailto:privacy@ruffus.app).
