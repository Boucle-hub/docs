## Get up and running in three steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create your card">
    Sign up at [ruffus.app](https://ruffus.app) and build your loyalty card — set your stamp count, reward name, logo, and brand colours. The whole process takes less than five minutes.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Display your QR code">
    Download your QR poster from the dashboard, print it, and place it somewhere visible — at the counter, on a table, or in your window.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Watch them return">
    Customers scan the code to collect stamps. When they're one stamp away from a reward, Ruffus sends them an automatic reminder — bringing them back through your door.
  </Step>
</Steps>
