Ruffus is a digital loyalty stamp card platform built for independent local businesses. Instead of printing paper punch cards or investing in expensive hardware, you get a fully branded digital card that customers collect on their phone — by scanning a single QR code you display at your location.

## Who Ruffus is for

Ruffus is designed for any customer-facing business that benefits from repeat visits. It works especially well for cafés, bakeries, restaurants, juice bars, yoga studios, barbershops, bookstores, and other neighbourhood shops where building a loyal regular clientele is the lifeblood of the business. If your customers visit more than once, Ruffus gives you a structured, automated way to reward that loyalty.

## Key features

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Zero hardware" icon="qrcode">
    There is nothing to install, no terminal to buy, and no POS system to connect. Display your printed QR poster and you're ready to go.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Works everywhere" icon="mobile">
    Customers can save their loyalty card to their browser as a PWA, or add it directly to Apple Wallet or Google Wallet — it always stays up to date.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Real-time analytics" icon="chart-line">
    Your dashboard shows visit trends, your top customers, stamp activity, and reward redemption rates so you always know how your program is performing.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Staff-verified stamps" icon="shield-check">
    Every stamp starts as a customer request and only lands once staff approve it in one tap — usually within seconds. That staff-in-the-loop check is what keeps rewards going only to real visits.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Automated reward alerts" icon="bell">
    The moment a customer completes their card, Ruffus sends them a "your reward is ready" email and push notification automatically. If they don't come back to claim it, a reminder follows — no effort on your part.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Win-back campaigns" icon="arrow-rotate-left">
    Customers who go quiet for 30+ days get an automatic win-back email and push nudge, with room for your own short personal note — all without you having to build or send anything.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Trilingual support

Ruffus supports **French, English, and Spanish (FR/EN/ES)**, with French as the default — built Québec-first from day one. The customer-facing card, merchant dashboard, transactional emails, and wallet passes are all available in all three languages, making Ruffus a natural fit for businesses serving Québec's French-speaking communities as well as English- and Spanish-speaking customers across Canada and beyond.
