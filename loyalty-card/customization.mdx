Your Ruffus loyalty card is a direct extension of your brand. Every element customers see — from your logo and colours to the reward they're working toward — is fully under your control, and any change you make takes effect immediately across every active customer card.

## What You Can Customize

| Element | Description |
|---|---|
| **Logo** | Uploaded image displayed at the top of the card |
| **Business name** | Shown on the card and in Wallet passes |
| **Brand colours** | Background and accent colour of the card |
| **Stamp count** | Number of stamps required to complete one reward cycle |
| **Reward name** | Short label for the reward (e.g. "Free Coffee") |
| **Reward description** | Optional detail shown to customers (e.g. "Any size, any drink") |

## Set Up a New Card

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the Card Builder">
    In your Ruffus dashboard, click **Loyalty Cards** in the left sidebar, then click **Create New Card**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter Your Business Details">
    Type your **business name** exactly as you want customers to see it on the card and in their Wallet.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Upload Your Logo">
    Click **Upload Logo** and select an image file (PNG or SVG recommended). Ruffus crops and scales it automatically — no editing required.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set Your Brand Colours">
    Click the colour swatch next to **Background Colour** and **Accent Colour** to open the colour picker. Enter a hex code or drag the slider to match your brand.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose Your Stamp Count">
    Enter the number of stamps a customer must collect to earn one reward. See the tip below for guidance on picking the right number.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Name Your Reward">
    Fill in the **Reward Name** field (e.g. "Free Slice") and, optionally, add a **Reward Description** to give customers more detail.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save and Publish">
    Click **Save Card**. Your card is live immediately — head to the **QR Code** tab to download your printable poster.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  **Choosing the right stamp count:** Most businesses see the best results with **6–10 stamps per reward cycle**. Fewer than 6 can feel too easy to abuse; more than 10 risks customers losing interest before they reach the reward. Coffee shops and quick-service venues typically thrive at 6–8, while higher-ticket businesses like salons or restaurants often do well at 8–10.
</Tip>

## Edit an Existing Card

You can update any card detail at any time without creating a new card.

1. Go to **Loyalty Cards** and click the card you want to update.
2. Click **Edit Card** in the top-right corner.
3. Change any field — logo, colours, reward name, or stamp count.
4. Click **Save Changes**.

Changes apply **immediately** to every customer's card. Customers who already have the card saved in Apple or Google Wallet will see the updated branding the next time their pass refreshes.

<Note>
  Changing the stamp count on an active card does **not** reset existing customer progress. A customer who has 4 stamps on an 8-stamp card will keep those 4 stamps if you change the goal to 10.
</Note>

## Create a Seasonal or Promotional Card

All Ruffus plans include **unlimited active cards**, so you can run a separate card for a seasonal promotion, a product launch, or a second location without affecting your main card.

1. Go to **Loyalty Cards** and click **Create New Card**.
2. Customize it for your promotion — different reward, different accent colour, shorter stamp count, or anything else that fits.
3. Download the new QR code poster and display it wherever the promotion applies.
4. When the promotion ends, click **Archive Card** to stop new stamps from being issued. Existing customer progress is preserved in case you reactivate it later.
