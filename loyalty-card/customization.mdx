Every loyalty card in Ruffus is designed in **Card Studio**, found at **Dashboard → QR Poster**. This is where you control how your card looks and how many stamps it takes to earn a reward. Changes are owner-only and apply immediately — no publishing step, no waiting.

The studio centres on a live preview of the card itself — the exact card your customers see when they tap your NFC station card or scan your QR code — and it updates in real time as you edit. Use the **Preview the tap** button to play the same stamp animation and confetti celebration your customers get at the counter. The printable QR poster and the Apple/Google Wallet previews sit in tabs beside the card preview.

<Note>
  Only the account owner can edit card design. Managers and staff can view the card but cannot change its appearance or reward rules.
</Note>

## Reward label in three languages

Ruffus is trilingual by default. When you set your reward — for example "Free coffee" — you write it once for each of French, English, and Spanish. Ruffus shows the customer the version that matches their chosen language automatically, on the card, in wallet passes, and in emails.

<Tip>
  Keep the reward label short and concrete. "Café gratuit," "Free coffee," and "Café gratis" all read cleanly on a small stamp card — long descriptions get cut off on some phone screens.
</Tip>

## Changes apply immediately

As soon as you save a change in Card Studio, it takes effect for every customer:

- The customer-facing card updates the next time it loads.
- Apple Wallet and Google Wallet passes already on customers' phones refresh automatically to reflect the new design.
- The QR poster reflects the new branding the next time you download or print it.

## Changing your stamp count

You can raise or lower the number of stamps required for a reward at any time. This never resets customer progress:

- **Raising the count** — customers keep every stamp they've already earned and simply need a few more to reach the new total.
- **Lowering the count** — any customer whose existing stamp total now meets or exceeds the new, lower threshold is retroactively granted their reward.

<Note>
  There's no "undo" on a stamp-count change beyond adjusting the number again — but because progress is never lost either direction, it's safe to experiment until the card feels right.
</Note>

## Running more than one card

Ruffus supports multiple loyalty cards side by side — each with its own name, stamp count, and reward label — managed from **Dashboard → Cards**. Your Card Studio branding (logo, colours, stamp style) is shared across all of them. See [Multiple Cards](/loyalty-card/multiple-cards) for how caps, enrollment, and stamping work.

For a limited-time promotion, such as bonus stamps for new members or double stamps at a specific location, you don't need a separate card — use **Campaigns**, which can be scoped by location, date range, and card.
