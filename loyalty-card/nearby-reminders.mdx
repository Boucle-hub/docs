Apple Wallet can surface a customer's loyalty card on their lock screen when they're physically near your shop — a gentle nudge to stop in and collect a stamp. Ruffus lets you turn this on per card.

## Turning it on

The **Nearby reminder** toggle lives in **Settings → Program rules**, per card. It's **off by default** — turn it on for any card where you want the reminder.

<Note>
  There's nothing for the customer to set up. If they've already added the card to Apple Wallet, the reminder activates automatically the next time their pass refreshes.
</Note>

## What to expect

- **Apple Wallet only.** Google Wallet has no equivalent feature — Google doesn't support geofenced passes, so this reminder never appears for customers using Google Wallet or the home-screen PWA.
- **The radius isn't configurable.** iOS decides how close a customer needs to be before the pass surfaces on their lock screen — roughly 100 metres. Ruffus can't widen or narrow that radius.
- **Uses your store's address.** Ruffus attaches the location(s) you have on file — up to 10 — to the pass so iOS knows where to trigger the reminder.
- **Changes apply on the next refresh, not instantly.** Turning the toggle on or off doesn't push immediately to passes already sitting in a customer's wallet — it takes effect the next time each pass refreshes, such as after their next stamp.

See [Multi-Location](/dashboard/multi-location) if you need to check or update the addresses on file for your locations, and [Wallet](/loyalty-card/wallet) for how Apple Wallet passes work more generally.
