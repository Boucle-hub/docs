Ruffus loyalty cards aren't limited to a browser tab. Customers can save their card to Apple Wallet, Google Wallet, or their phone's home screen as a Progressive Web App (PWA) — whichever fits how they use their phone.

## Adding the card to a wallet

The **Add to Apple Wallet** and **Add to Google Wallet** buttons are available on the customer card page right away — customers don't need to earn a stamp first to save their card.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Apple Wallet">
    Works on any iPhone with Apple Wallet. Tapping **Add to Apple Wallet** installs the pass in one step.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Google Wallet">
    Works on Android with the Google Wallet app. Tapping **Add to Google Wallet** installs the pass in one step.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

<Note>
  There's no specific OS version requirement to worry about — if the customer's phone can run Apple Wallet or Google Wallet, their Ruffus card will work in it.
</Note>

## Passes stay up to date automatically

Once a card is added to a wallet, it refreshes on its own — no action needed from the customer:

- **Every stamp and every redemption** updates the pass automatically.
- **Apple Wallet** shows a lock-screen change message — "New stamp added" — whenever the pass updates, so customers notice without opening the app.
- **Google Wallet** updates the pass silently in the background; it doesn't show a system notification the way Apple's pass does.

## Nearby reminder (Apple Wallet)

Apple Wallet passes can also surface on a customer's lock screen when they're physically near your shop — a built-in nearby reminder, off by default. Turn it on per card from **Settings → Program rules**. See [Nearby Reminders](/loyalty-card/nearby-reminders) for how it works and its limits.

## Real notifications customers actually get

Wallet passes update quietly, but Ruffus also sends real notifications (email and push) at two moments:

1. **Reward ready** — the instant a customer completes their card, they get an email and push notification letting them know their reward is waiting.
2. **Reward reminder** — if an earned reward sits unclaimed for 14 or more days, Ruffus sends an automatic follow-up email and push notification.

<Note>
  These are separate from the Apple Wallet lock-screen message. The reward-ready and reminder notifications go out over email and push regardless of whether the customer has added their card to a wallet at all.
</Note>

## Progressive Web App (PWA)

Customers who'd rather not use a wallet app can add their loyalty card straight to their phone's home screen:

- **Add to Home Screen** creates an app-like icon that opens directly to their card — no browser chrome, no typing in a URL.
- The card is **offline-tolerant**, so a spotty connection at checkout doesn't break the experience.
- It supports **web push**, so the reward-ready and reminder notifications above work the same way as on a native wallet pass.

This makes the PWA a solid option for customers on phones or browsers that don't play well with Apple or Google Wallet.
