title: "Quickstart Guide: Launch Your Loyalty Card in 5 Minutes"
sidebarTitle: "Quickstart"
description: "Create your Ruffus account, build your branded loyalty card, and collect your first customer stamp — all in under five minutes, no hardware needed."
---

This guide walks you through everything you need to go from zero to a live loyalty program. By the end of the four steps below, your card will be active and ready for your first customer scan.

<Note>
  Customers never need to download an app. Your loyalty card opens instantly in any mobile browser the moment a customer scans your QR code — and they can optionally save it to Apple Wallet or Google Wallet for quick access later.
</Note>

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create your account">
    Go to [ruffus.app/auth/signup](https://ruffus.app/auth/signup) and sign up for a free account. Your 14-day free trial starts immediately — no credit card required. You get full access to every feature during the trial so you can evaluate the platform with real customers before choosing a plan.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create your loyalty card">
    From your dashboard, click **Create card** and configure your card:

    - **Stamp count** — choose how many stamps customers need to earn a reward (for example, 8 stamps for a free coffee).
    - **Reward name** — describe exactly what the customer receives (for example, "Free oat milk latte").
    - **Logo** — upload your business logo; it appears at the top of every customer's card.
    - **Colours** — pick your brand colours so the card feels like a natural extension of your business.

    The entire setup takes less than five minutes. Your card is live the moment you save it.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Get your QR poster">
    Navigate to **Dashboard → QR Poster**, then click **Download**. Print the poster and place it somewhere visible — on your counter, at the entrance, or on your tables. Every scan of this code is tied securely to your card, so there is no risk of duplicate or fraudulent stamps.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Your first customer scans">
    When a customer points their phone camera at your QR code, here is what happens:

    1. Their browser opens the loyalty card instantly — no app download, no account needed.
    2. A verified stamp is added to their card in real time.
    3. The card is updated immediately, whether they're viewing it in a browser, as a saved PWA, or in Apple or Google Wallet.

    Once they reach the required stamp count, their card shows the reward and you can redeem it with one tap in your merchant terminal.
  </Step>
</Steps>
