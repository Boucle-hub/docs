This guide walks you through everything you need to go from zero to a live loyalty program. By the end of the four steps below, your card will be active and ready for your first customer scan.

<Note>
  Customers never need to download an app. The first time someone scans your QR code, they sign in once with just a phone number or email (or Google/Apple) — everything else happens right in their mobile browser, and they can optionally save the card to Apple Wallet or Google Wallet for quick access later.
</Note>

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create your account">
    Go to [ruffus.app/auth/signup](https://ruffus.app/auth/signup) and sign up for a free account. Your 14-day free trial starts immediately — no credit card required. You get full access to every feature during the trial so you can evaluate the platform with real customers before choosing a plan.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Design your card in Card Studio">
    From your dashboard, go to **Dashboard → QR Poster** (this is your **Card Studio**) and configure your card:

    - **Logo and brand colour** — upload your logo and pick a colour so the card feels like a natural extension of your business.
    - **Stamp shape** — choose from circle, square, triangle, diamond, star, hexagon, or heart, and set the fill icon and colour.
    - **Stamp count** — choose how many stamps customers need to earn a reward (for example, 8 stamps for a free coffee).
    - **Reward label** — describe exactly what the customer receives, in French, English, and Spanish.

    The entire setup takes less than five minutes. Your card is live the moment you save it, and you can always come back to adjust the stamp count or reward label later — customers never lose their progress when you do.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Get your QR poster">
    Still in **Dashboard → QR Poster**, download your poster as a PNG, or print it straight from your browser's print dialog. Place it somewhere visible — on your counter, at the entrance, or on your tables. The QR code encodes a permanent link, so it never needs to be reprinted or reissued.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Approve your first stamp">
    When a customer scans your QR code, here is what happens:

    1. Their browser opens the loyalty card and asks them to sign in — a phone number or email is all it takes, no app download.
    2. This creates a stamp request with a short, roughly 30-second window.
    3. You (or your staff) get a push notification and approve it in one tap. The stamp then lands on the customer's card instantly.

    That one-tap approval is intentional: it's what keeps every stamp tied to a real visit. Your welcome package includes a staff QR code you can scan to open the staff portal on any phone, tablet, or computer — so you can start approving stamp requests immediately, with no extra setup.
  </Step>
</Steps>
