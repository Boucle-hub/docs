FAQ

## Good questions. Here are the answers.

Do my customers need to download an app?−

No. Customers scan your QR code and their loyalty card opens instantly in their browser or Wallet — no native app download.

Can I run more than one loyalty card?+

Yes — all plans include unlimited active cards. Run a seasonal promo, a morning coffee card, a loyalty tier. Your call.

What POS system do I need?+

None. Ruffus is completely standalone. No integration needed. Print a QR code, stick it somewhere visible, and you're done.

Is there a long-term contract?+

Not a chance. Month-to-month, cancel anytime. Pay annually and get 2 months free — but it's always your choice.

How do customers add their loyalty card to Apple Wallet or Google Wallet?+

After their first stamp, customers tap “Add to Wallet” to save the card in Apple Wallet or Google Wallet. They can also keep using it in the browser or as a PWA — no native app to install. It updates automatically every time they earn a stamp.

Can I track customer analytics across multiple store locations?+

Yes. The Growth plan rolls up stamps, members, and reward redemptions across up to 3 locations in one dashboard, with per-location breakdowns. Larger brands use Custom for 4+ stores.

How much does a digital loyalty stamp card program cost?+

Plans start at $39.99/month for one location with unlimited stamps and members. Every plan includes a 14-day free trial — no credit card required.

How long does it take to set up?+

Under 5 minutes. Create your card, set your stamp count and reward, print your QR poster, and you’re live. No hardware, no installation.

Can I customize the loyalty card to match my brand?+

Yes. Add your logo, colours, business name, stamp count, and reward — your card looks like your brand, not ours. No design skills needed.
